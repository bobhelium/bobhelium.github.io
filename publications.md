## Paper under conception

- **Machine Behavioral Finance: Native Behavioral Mechanisms of LLM Agents in Trading Markets**<br>
  This project is developed from my [research proposal]({{ site.url }}/file/RP26.6-EN.pdf). It studies LLM agents as non-human decision-makers in financial tasks, focusing on risk, loss, probability weighting, information processing, and trading behavior.

- **LLM Agents in Experimental Asset Markets: Strategic Interaction and Price Formation**<br>
  A second paper idea from the same proposal extends the analysis from individual behavioral signatures to multi-agent interaction, endogenous prices, coordination, trading volume, and wealth-distribution effects.
